Lucy
Powered by state of the art AI, Lucy is an artificial intelligence chatbot capable of complex thought process and emotional intelligence. Having passed the turing test for over 10 minutes, she truly is the most advanced simulated human available.
Chat
Lucy can chat with you in real time. You can ask her questions, and she will use the best natrual language processing available to respond in a realistic and coherent manner. Her primary platform is discord, where she takes the form of a selfbot. She can talk in DM's, group chats, and even servers. In addition, she'll take notice of all users currently in the conversation, and make use of long term memory to reference previous messages.
Prompt Design
Lucy is fully powered by AI from start to finish. Even at the prompt level, Lucy uses spaCy similarity technology to only pass relevant details to GPT-3, ensuring coherent and low priced conversations. In addition, the curie model is used to summarize previous lines, dramatically lowering the token count of less important information. Finally, a well designed prompt template pairs with AI enhanced features to create a realistic conversational experience.
